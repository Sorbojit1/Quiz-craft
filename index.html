About
Quizcraft.

Quizcraft is a passion project — a fully client-side quiz builder and player built with pure HTML, CSS, and vanilla JavaScript. No frameworks, no backend, no tracking. Just a slick tool that works, saved to your browser.

S

Sorbojit

Creator & developer of Quizcraft

@Sorbojit1

The mission

Most quiz builders are either too simple or locked behind sign-ups and paywalls. Quizcraft was built to be genuinely useful, free, and satisfying to use.

Everything runs in your browser. Nothing is sent to a server. Your quizzes are yours.

It started as a basic card maker and grew into a full quiz platform — timers, scoring, analytics, a library, themes, sounds, and more.

What's included

  • 4 question types (MCQ, T/F, text, ordering)
  • Per-question timers, hints, images, points
  • Save & manage unlimited quizzes
  • Export / import as portable JSON
  • Detailed score analytics & play history
  • 3 colour themes (dark / light / neon)
  • Keyboard shortcuts for everything
  • Confetti on a perfect score 🎉
  • Drag-to-reorder questions
  • Undo deleted questions
